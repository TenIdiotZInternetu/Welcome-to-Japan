Prefectures of Japan 都道府県

Japan is divided into 47 prefectures (都道府県, todōfuken), which rank immediately below the national government. They include 43 prefectures proper (県, ken), two urban prefectures (府, fu: Osaka and Kyoto), one "circuit" or "territory" (道, dō: Hokkai-dō) and one metropolis (都, to: Tokyo)
